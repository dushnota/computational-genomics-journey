Computational Genomics Journey

About Me

I am an 18-year-old student from Russia who wants to become a geneticist.

I am preparing to study General Medicine at Pacific State Medical University (ТГМУ). In the future, I want to develop toward genetics and computational genomics.

At the moment, I am practically starting from zero in programming, data analysis, and computational genomics. This repository is the beginning of my long-term journey.

Why Computational Genomics?

Modern biology and genetics increasingly depend on computation, programming, and data analysis.

I want to understand how biological questions can be explored using data, code, and computational methods.

My long-term path is:

Genetics → Biology → Programming → Data Analysis → Computational Genomics

I do not expect to become a bioinformatician in a few weeks. My goal is to build a strong foundation step by step.
